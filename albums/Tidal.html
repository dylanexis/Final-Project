The First Taste

This is an unbelievably emotional piece of music to come from 19 year old Fiona Apple. Her first studio album delves into her feelings of loning and forlornment, as well as mistakes that she has made herself in her relationships. I return to this album continuously because of its beautiful production and Apple's captivating vocals. That girl can play the piano!!

@Dylan James 2024
